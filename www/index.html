Clean Air

The quality of the air we breathe has a dramatic impact on our region's overall lung health. Our Clean Air Programs are designed help prevent local air pollution through education and increased community awareness.


Clean Air Programs

Healthy Lungs

Asthma is one of the most prevalent lung diseases in the Greater Sacramento Region. Our Asthma Programs empower asthma sufferers to proactively and effectively manage their asthma symptoms.


Asthma Programs

Tobacco-Free Future

Tobacco is the leading cause of preventable lung disease. Our tobacco programs seek to prevent tobacco initiation among youth and young adults, protect nonsmokers from toxic second- and third-hand smoke in their homes, schools and workplaces, expose tobacco industry targeting tactics and provide cessation resources to those who want to quit.
