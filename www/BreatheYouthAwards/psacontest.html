#BeTheFirst Tobacco-Free Generation Video Contest

Breathe California is hosting the third annual Anti-Tobacco Video Contest as part of the Breathe Youth Media Awards Ceremony. The contest is open to Middle High and High School students in California and the winners are selected by Breathe California’s own Youth Advisory Board. Winners will be announced at the Youth Media Awards and receive cash prizes. Check out samples of past winners below!

This Year's Theme:

#BeTheFirst: What would it be like if your generation were the first to be tobacco-free?
