Current Volunteer Opportunities at BREATHE

At BREATHE, we LOVE volunteers! As a locally focused non-profit organization, we largely depend on the support of our dedicated volunteers to keep our programs and advocacy efforts moving forward.

If you have time and talent to share, and would like to use it in our efforts to achieve clean air, healthier lungs and a tobacco-free future in the Sacramento region, please consider sharing those resources with us!


Current Volunteer Opportunities

  • General Office Assistant - copying, filing, assembling information packets & quit kits, etc.
  • Special Event Volunteer - help us with variety of public-facing and/or behind-the-scenes tasks at our annual events
  • Communications Volunteer - contribute social media content, general web maintenance, photography, design, etc.
