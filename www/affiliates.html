Breathe California of Sacramento-Emigrant Trails

Breathe California of Sacramento-Emigrant Trails services 28 Northern California counties including: Alpine, Amador, Butte, Calaveras, Colusa, El Dorado, Glenn, Lake, Lassen, Mariposa, Modoc, Mono, Nevada, Placer, Plumas, Sacramento, San Joaqui, Shasta, Sierra, Siskiyou, Solano, Stanislaus, Sutter, Tehama, Trinity, Tuolumne, Yolo, and Yuba.


Statewide Breathe California Affiliates
