  • How does my company become a sponsor or community partner?

    You can support our organization and mission by sponsoring or partnering with us on one of our signature events: the Breathe Bike Trek, Clean Air Awards, or the Breathe Youth Media Awards. You can also sponsor or partner with us on any of our clean air, asthma, or tobacco-free programs. Click here to learn more about becoming a corporate sponsor.

  • What else can my company do to support BREATHE's work?

    Besides sponsorship and partnership opportunities, your company can volunteer or provide an in-kind donation.
